	\subsection*{Part  2: Building Expressions}
	Give regular expressions for each of the following languages. Simplify as much as you can. Excessively complicated expressions might not get full points. No proofs required, but if explaining your
	regular expression might help the reader see what’s going on, feel free.
	1. All strings $ x $ over $ \{a, b\} $ with an even number of $ a $’s.
	2. All strings $ x $ over $ {a, b} $ with $ \#a(x) \equiv \#b(x)(mod 2) $.
	3. All strings over $ \{a, b\} $ that do not contain the (contiguous) substring $ aba $.
	
	\linesep
	
	\begin{enumerate}[1.]
		\item $ (ab^* a+b)^* $
		\item $ ((a+b)(a+b))^* $
		\item $ (a^*b+ba^*)^*+a^* $
	\end{enumerate}
